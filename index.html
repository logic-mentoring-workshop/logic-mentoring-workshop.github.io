Logic Mentoring Workshop

Overview

The Logic Mentoring Workshops (LMWs) introduce young researchers to the technical and practical aspects of a career in logic research. They are targeted at students, from senior undergraduates to graduates, and include talks and panel sessions from leaders in the subject.
